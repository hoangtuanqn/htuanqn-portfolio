Products

CheckScam

Live
Scam/fraud verification for Vietnam's MMO & gaming community

Online scam is a real, recurring cost in Vietnam's MMO and gaming community - buyers and sellers lose money to fraud with no easy way to check who they're dealing with. CheckScam lets users look up a seller/trader before transacting and report scammers to warn others, turning scattered scam reports into one searchable record.

  • Aggregates and reconciles scam-report data from multiple independent sources into a single lookup.
  • Community-reporting flow so new scam cases get added and surfaced without manual moderation for every entry.
Laravel MySQL Third-party data integration

Live: checkscam.tuanori.vn

ShopAccGame

Live
Game account marketplace with integrated payments

A marketplace for buying and selling game accounts, where the core risk is the same as any C2C trade: payment and delivery need to settle safely without either side getting scammed. Currently expanding the "Khu Mua Bán" (trading zone) feature to widen what can be listed and traded on the platform.

  • Integrated with the SePay payment gateway for real-time payment confirmation.
  • Built a prepaid card top-up flow so users can fund their balance without a bank transfer each time.
Laravel MySQL SePay payment gateway
